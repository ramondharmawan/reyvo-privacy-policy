# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 26 January 2026

## Overview
Reyvo is a speech-to-text application that runs on your device and does not require user accounts or registration.

## Data Collection
Reyvo does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

## Microphone Access
Reyvo uses microphone access solely to convert speech into text while the app is in use.
Audio recordings are not saved, and transcribed text is not transmitted to our servers.

## Speech Recognition Processing
Speech-to-text functionality is provided using the device’s built-in speech recognition service.
Depending on the device, language, and system configuration, speech recognition may require an internet connection and may be processed by the device’s speech recognition provider (such as Google or the device manufacturer).

## Third-Party Services
Reyvo does not use analytics, advertising, tracking, or crash reporting services.

## Changes to This Policy
This Privacy Policy may be updated if application functionality changes. Any updates will be published on this page.

## Contact
If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you may contact the developer via the email address listed on the Google Play Store.
